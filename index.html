A Chrsitian's Questions for Christians

A webiste that goes through the entire Bible and lists the questions I, and probably many others, have about The Bible. These questions will be an open forum debate until we come to a conclusion using Biblical evidence.

Why are you doing this? Because I am a college student who is asked to explain why I believe what I believe and I am not prepared or equipped with the correct knowledge to answer these questions. This is meant to help others in my situation and to also help me understand Christianity. I think if I am to devote my life to something then I must be able to explain it to someone else.
